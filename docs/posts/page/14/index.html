Like a naughty child, confined to its room, watching his friends from the window as they play outside, the UK public have become increasingly frustrated. Although the outbreak of coronavirus is a global pandemic, nations around the world have responded differently with countries such as Germany and New Zealand having relatively more success compared to the UK, America and Brazil who have experienced the highest death tolls.
