#FLOFavourite: Boca Cha Cha

What’s the deal ? Boca Cha Cha is an eccentric bohemian café and restaurant tucked away in Little Venice. With an eclectic mash-up of velvet sofas, disco balls, wicker armchairs, neon lights and chandeliers, and contemporary art the décor is as much a draw as the food. Boca Cha Cha brings together a fusion of Mexican, Mediterranean, and Spanish foods, from cheese and truffle croquet with poached egg to French toast with dulce de leche and berries.
