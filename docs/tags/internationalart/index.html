The Mediterranean will be at the heart of ARCOmadrid 2023

The 42nd edition of ARCOmadrid, the fair organised by IFEMA MADRID, will be held from 22 -26 February 2023 with the Mediterranean as its core project. Image: courtesy of ARCOmadrid ARCOmadrid 2023 curated by Marina Fokidis with the advice of Bouchra Khalili and Hila Peleg will revolve around the art scenes of the countries surrounding it, from south to north and coast to coast. The curatorial team will present a series of artists and galleries from the countries of this rich and complex geography.
